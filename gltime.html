About Accuracy

This application uses your device's GPS for maximum accuracy. The time calculation is based on your longitude and uses timezone data to provide precise local time.

For best results:

  • Enable GPS/location services on your device
  • Ensure you have a clear view of the sky for GPS signals
  • Allow the browser to access your location when prompted
